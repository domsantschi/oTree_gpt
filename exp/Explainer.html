Your task is to interact with your virtual colleagues. Each virtual colleague holds valuable information about a specific position of the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) Budget. Your goal is to collect and piece together COGS-related insights by engaging with them strategically.

  • Move your character through the 3D space to approach the virtual colleagues - use Arrow keys to move your character
  • Type and press Enter to chat. Conversations with virtual colleagues will only trigger when your character is close enough (within 10 units).
  • Each virtual colleague specializes in a specific COGS domain:
    • Red Bot: Specialist in Direct Materials
    • Black Bot: Specialist in Direct Manufacturing Labor.
    • Green Bot: Specialist in Manufacturing Overhead.

  • Ask questions relevant to their expertise to gather useful insights. If you ask something outside their expertise, they will suggest consulting another virtual colleague.
  • You will have 1 minute to gather as much information as possible.

After the time is up, you will need to make a decision based on the information you have collected.

Use this opportunity to explore the environment and interact strategically to uncover the truth. Good luck!
