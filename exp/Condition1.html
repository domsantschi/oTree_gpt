Congratulations on answering the knowledge questions correctly!

You are now allowed to proceed with the study. Your manager will evaluate your performance and determine your compensation based on two factors:

{% if condition == 'Cost Impact Focus' %}
  • The Actual Costs of Goods Sold for the Year Ending December 31, 2025. The lower the actual costs, the better.
  • The Deviance between the Actual Costs of Goods Sold and the Budgeted Costs of Goods Sold for the Year Ending December 31, 2025. The lower the deviance, the better.

Both factors are equally weighted for your performance evaluation (both at 50%).

{% elif condition == 'Environmental Impact Focus' %}
  • The Actual CO2 Emissions of the Costs of Goods Sold for the Year Ending December 31, 2025. The lower the actual CO2 emissions, the better.
  • The Deviance between the Actual CO2 Emissions of the Costs of Goods Sold and the Budgeted CO2 Emissions of the Costs of Goods Sold for the Year Ending December 31, 2025. The lower the deviance, the better..

Both factors are equally weighted for your performance evaluation (both at 50%).
