How Points are Determined

Your payoff in each round depends on what action you choose and what action the other participant chooses. Here is the complete payoff table:

The Other Participant
Action 1 Action 2
You Action 1 {{ C.BOTH_COOPERATE_PAYOFF }}, {{ C.BOTH_COOPERATE_PAYOFF }} {{ C.BETRAYED_PAYOFF }}, {{ C.BETRAY_PAYOFF }}
Action 2 {{ C.BETRAY_PAYOFF }}, {{ C.BETRAYED_PAYOFF }} {{ C.BOTH_DEFECT_PAYOFF }}, {{ C.BOTH_DEFECT_PAYOFF }}

(Your payoff is listed first, your partner's payoff is listed second)

Example: If you choose Action 1 and your partner chooses Action 1, you both receive {{ C.BOTH_COOPERATE_PAYOFF }} points. If you choose Action 2 and your partner chooses Action 1, you receive {{ C.BETRAY_PAYOFF }} points and they receive {{ C.BETRAYED_PAYOFF }} points.
