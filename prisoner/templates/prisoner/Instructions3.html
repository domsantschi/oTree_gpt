How Long Will the Game Last?

The number of rounds is not predetermined. Instead, after each round:

  • There is a 90% chance the game continues to another round
  • There is a 10% chance the game ends

Each round's continuation is determined independently by a random draw. This means you cannot predict exactly how many rounds you will play.

Important: You will stay paired with the same participant throughout all rounds.
Your Goal

Your goal is to accumulate as many points as possible across all rounds. Your total earnings will be the sum of your payoffs from every round you play.

At the end of the game, you will be asked to complete a short questionnaire about your experience.
