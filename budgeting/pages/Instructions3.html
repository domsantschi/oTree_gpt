The Negotiation Process

Each round focuses on a different budget account. You will alternate between expense accounts (like Education, Healthcare) and revenue accounts (like Property Taxes, Grants).

Per Round:
  1. Review Data: See historical trends and target amounts for the current account
  2. Make Proposals: Each player proposes a budget amount simultaneously
  3. Negotiate: Adjust your proposals in real-time until both players agree
  4. Score: Points awarded based on how close your agreement is to the target
Agreement Process

To complete a round, both players must click "I Agree" on the same proposal amounts. You can continue adjusting your proposals until you reach mutual agreement.

Time Pressure: The entire negotiation lasts exactly 5 minutes. Try to complete as many rounds as possible!
Communication

You negotiate only through your budget proposals - no direct chat or messaging with your partner.
