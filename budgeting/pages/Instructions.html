Please read the following instructions carefully.

Note: To get started and familiarize yourself with the scenario, there will be a knowledge task. Then, you will complete a budgeting task.

  • • In this task, you will complete a budgeting task for a public expense account.
  • • You will analyze historical expense data, set your budget, and justify your decision.
  • • After submitting your budget, you will receive feedback and decide whether to revise your submission.
  • • Your budget consists of two components: a best estimate (your estimate of the most likely expense) and a buffer (additional amount for uncertainty).
  • • Your goal is to set a budget that is as close as possible to the actual expenses.

Bonus Prize: The participant with the highest quality response (based on both the budget accuracy and the justifications) will receive an additional cash prize of CHF 50.
