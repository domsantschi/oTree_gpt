In each round, you will:

  1. Analyze historical data for the expense category
  2. Submit a budget forecast with a best estimate and uncertainty buffer
  3. Justify your decision in writing
  4. Review advice from an expert source
  5. Decide whether to revise your submission based on the advice
  6. See the results - the actual expenses will be revealed and compared to your forecast
üí° Understanding the Budget

For each expense category, you will set a budget consisting of two components:

  • Best Estimate: Your forecast of the most likely expense amount
  • Buffer for Uncertainty: An additional amount added to your estimate to account for potential variations

Final Budget = Best Estimate + Buffer

The historical data will show you how budgeted vs. actual expenses have compared over the past 10 years.

üéØ Your Goal

Your goal is to set accurate budgets that are as close as possible to the actual expenses.

After each round, you will see how your forecast compared to:

  • The actual expenses that occurred
  • The expert advisor's recommendation

At the end, you'll see your overall accuracy across all three rounds.
