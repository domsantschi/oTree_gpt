Welcome to this study!

In this study, you will be paired with another participant. Together, you will play a series of decision-making rounds.

How it works

In each round, both you and your partner will simultaneously choose between two actions: Action 1 or Action 2. Your payoff depends on both your choice and your partner's choice.

Game Duration

After each round, there is a 90% chance that the game continues to another round, and a 10% chance that the game ends.

This means you do not know in advance how many rounds you will play. Each round's continuation is determined independently by a random draw.

Your Goal

Your goal is to accumulate as many points as possible across all rounds. At the end, you will be asked to complete a short questionnaire about your experience.
