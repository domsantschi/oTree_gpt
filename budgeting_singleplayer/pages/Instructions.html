Study Overview

In this study, you will take on a role in a public budgeting scenario. Your task is to:

  1. Submit a budget forecast for a Public Services expense account
  2. Decide on a budget adjustment (increase or decrease from your forecast)
  3. Justify your decision in writing
  4. Review advice from an expert source
  5. Decide whether to revise your initial submission based on the advice
Budget Context

You will be working with a Public Services expense account for a Swiss municipality. This account covers essential services provided to citizens.

You will see:

  • Historical expense data from the past 5 years
  • The current year's actual expenses

Your task is to forecast next year's expenses and set an appropriate budget.

Incentive

Win a CHF 20 Voucher!

Participants who submit the best justifications for their budget decisions will be entered into a drawing to win a CHF 20 ProCity St.Gallen voucher, which can be used at various shops in St.Gallen.

Your justifications will be evaluated based on:

  • Clarity and coherence of reasoning
  • Consideration of relevant factors
  • Quality of argumentation

Please take time to provide thoughtful explanations for your decisions.
