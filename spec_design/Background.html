Livit Tech is developing an End of Life Care Machine, an automated healthcare solution designed to provide comfort and support during patients' final moments. The technology includes:

  • Advanced voice interaction systems for patient comfort
  • Robotic assistance capabilities for physical support
  • Automated care delivery mechanisms

According to the Lead Designer, Dan Chen, this innovative yet sensitive healthcare automation project represents a significant technological advancement in patient care. However, it also raises important questions about the intersection of healthcare automation, patient dignity, and ethical care delivery.
